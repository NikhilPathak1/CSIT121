Welcome to Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia. It is known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. Here's some information about Nepal's tourism:

Tourist Attractions

Place Description
Kathmandu Valley The capital city, Kathmandu, is home to ancient temples and palaces.
Mount Everest The world's highest peak attracts trekkers and climbers from around the world.
Chitwan National Park Explore the wildlife, including tigers, rhinos, and elephants.

Why Visit Nepal?

  • Experience breathtaking mountain views.
  • Explore diverse cultures and traditions.
  • Enjoy outdoor activities like trekking and rafting.
