About Nepal

Area of Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia and has a total area of approximately 147,516 square kilometers.

Map of Nepal

Capital City

Kathmandu is the capital and largest city of Nepal, situated in the Kathmandu Valley in the central part of the country.

Crazy Facts About Nepal

- Nepal is home to eight of the world's 14 highest peaks, including Mount Everest, the highest point on Earth.
- The country is rich in cultural diversity with numerous ethnic groups and languages.
-There are many world heritage Sites in nepa. Birthplace of Lord Buddha, Lumbini, is one of UNESCO World Heritage Site in Nepal.
