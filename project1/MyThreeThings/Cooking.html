Indian Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 50ml mustard oil
  • 1 lbs regular chicken thigs, cut into comfortable pieces
  • 1 chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated or you can used garlic ginger paste
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup plain yogurt to increase taste
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powsder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt according to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a large thicker skillet, heat the mustard oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and stir until they turn little browniss colour.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet. Cook for another 2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken cube and fry for like a minute.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Stir in the curry powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook for a couple of minutes to toast the spices.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the yogurt. Cover for 10-15 minutes, stirring often make sure, until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  8. Serve your delicious chicken curry with steamed rice or naan bread.

Why This is My Favorite Recipe:

My all-time favourite chicken curry recipe incorporates tender chicken pieces with the complex, addictive flavours of Indian spices. A delicious and satisfying dish is produced when the spicy heat of the spices is perfectly balanced by the creamy yogurt-based sauce. I enjoy cooking this hearty dish for friends and family because it makes me feel good and satisfied.
